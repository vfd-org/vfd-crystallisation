## The undismissable spine

> **The 0.88 % match.** Two axioms (self-similarity, E₈ maximality) plus standard classical mathematics (Banach, Coxeter, Schläfli, Elkies, Burago–Ivanov, Fierz–Pauli, Deser 1970, Birkhoff) yield Λ·ℓ_P² = 2·φ⁻⁵⁸³ ≈ 2.892×10⁻¹²² with no free parameter at this stage of the derivation. Planck 2018 measures 2.845–2.889 ×10⁻¹²². Discrepancy: 0.88 %, smaller than the Hubble-tension systematic between Planck CMB and SH0ES local determinations. The cascade derivation predicts H₀=68.83 km/s/Mpc, sitting between Planck (67.36) and SH0ES (73.04). It predicts Ω_Λ=2/3, w=−1 exactly, H₀·t₀=0.951 (observed 0.952), and the gauge-invariant Friedmann combination H₀·√Ω_Λ=56.20 km/s/Mpc (Planck observed 55.75, gap 0.81 %).

A reader who rejects the narrative still has to either (i) explain the 0.88 % match as a five-coincidence pile-up, or (ii) deny one of the cited classical theorems. Both are heavy lifts.
