## 1. Purpose of This WO

The adult-brain empirical thread — DMN (default mode network),
EEG signatures, polytope-substrate neuroscience preregistrations —
does **not** live in `papers/biology-rung/`. It lives in a sibling
programme that is **more mature than this one**:

> `aria-chess/docs/brain_mapping/` — polytope-substrate
> neuroscience, **15/18 preregistered hits**, MANUSCRIPT and
> X_NARRATIVE artefacts ready for rollout (per project memory as of
> 2026-04-22).

A user reading this programme might reasonably expect "cascade
prediction for brain EEG / DMN" to fall out of the biology-rung
work, given that the cascade Life-rung substrate is shared with
adult-brain dynamics. The correct answer is:

- **The biology-rung programme does not re-derive the aria-chess
  brain-mapping results**; that would duplicate mature work.
- **Forward readers there** for any DMN / EEG / brain-mapping
  empirical content.
- **Use the biology-rung catalogue-of-math as a foundation** that
  the aria-chess thread can cite upstream when its math roots
  need to be tightened.

This WO exists so future biology-rung WOs do not accidentally open
brain-mapping work that is already running in the sibling programme,
and so the biology-rung paper trail explicitly references the
empirical neuroscience thread rather than pretending it doesn't exist.

## 3. When to Route Work Here vs. There

### Work routes to aria-chess (not biology-rung) when:

- The empirical target is adult human (or non-human primate) brain
  activity measurable via EEG, MEG, fMRI, DTI, etc.
- The claim concerns DMN, task networks, state-space dynamics of
  conscious experience, or cortical network topology.
- The prediction is downstream of a neuroscientific preregistration.

### Work routes to biology-rung (not aria-chess) when:

- The empirical target is molecular / sub-cellular / developmental:
  protein structure, capsid geometry, amyloid fibrils, microtubule
  lattices, embryonic morphogenesis.
- The claim concerns the geometric / symmetry content of biological
  structures rather than functional brain dynamics.

### Work that could route either way:

- **Levin-style bioelectric morphogenesis** (planarian regeneration,
  Xenopus electric face, bioelectric prepatterns). This sits in
  biology-rung as WO-4 because the target is **developmental
  biology** (morphogenetic attractor states), not adult brain
  dynamics. If the cascade-neuroscience / cascade-morphogenesis
  boundary ever needs a sharper distinction, this is where the two
  programmes meet.
