## 2. D₄ / 24 rung as SU(5) GUT adjoint

- D₄ = so(8), rank 4, dim 28 (not 24). The 24 refers to roots (non-Cartan elements), not full dimension.
- SU(5) = A₄, rank 4, dim 24.

So the **match at 24 is root-count to dim-count**, not Lie-algebra isomorphism.

- 24 fundamental-invariant-degree value (d_6 of E₈)

- F₄: 48 roots (nope)

Only SU(5) and SO(8) have 24 roots. Of these, SU(5) is the natural GUT candidate (Georgi-Glashow), and SO(8) is the triality group.

### Conjecture

> The D₄ rung carries both SU(5) gauge content AND D₄ triality simultaneously, via a natural identification of the 24-cell vertices with SU(5) root directions modulated by D₄ triality action.

This would explain the cascade's D₄ rung as the GUT gauge-boson generator space. Triality (the S₃ symmetry of D₄) would act as generation-mixing at the gauge level.

### What this gives us

The most natural interpretation — given the cascade's chirality / Z/2 center structure — is **left-handed Pati-Salam** where SU(2)_L is the weak group and SU(4) is colour + lepton unification. The right-handed SU(2)_R is absent from the center but may be a natural sub-structure to be derived.

So **Z/2 × Z/4 matches Z(Spin(10) × Z/2)**. Spin(10) is the GUT group SO(10) (covering group). Z/2 could be a parity factor.

The exponent group Z/2 × Z/4 is consistent with:
