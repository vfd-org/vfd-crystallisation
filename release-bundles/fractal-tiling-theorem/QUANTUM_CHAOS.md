## What this means — and doesn't
- **Real bridge:** the prime/zero "harmonics" obey the *same statistics as
  real, measured quantum chaotic spectra*. This is the genuine,
  experimentally-grounded place the mathematics connects to physics, and the
  strongest evidence for the Hilbert–Pólya picture (the zeros are spectral).
- **Not a proof:** statistics ≠ the operator; n=40 low zeros is indicative,
  not a precision fit (the decisive match is at very high zeros — Odlyzko,
  billions).
- **The bridge runs through quantum spectra — NOT suns, planets, or people.**
  There is no measured connection between primes and astrophysical or human
  objects (planetary-spacing numerology fails); that remains a mystic bridge
  with no empirical support. The real, testable physics is random-matrix /
  quantum-chaos spectra.

## How you'd "pit it against measurement" for real
Compare zero statistics (or a substrate L-function's high zeros) to: (i)
random-matrix predictions (GUE pair correlation, spacing, number variance);
(ii) measured nuclear level spacings; (iii) chaotic microwave-cavity
resonance statistics; (iv) proposed cold-atom / trapped-ion realisations of
the Berry–Keating xp Hamiltonian. All of these are legitimate, falsifiable
physical arenas. None involve planets or people.
