# Image brief — the geometry leading up to the wall

For each image: **the math** (what it actually depicts) + a **paste-ready prompt**
for the OpenAI app. These are *conceptual / intuition* images (art), not the rigorous
plots — the accurate figures (spacing histograms, number variance, 600-cell
projection) are the computed PNGs in `../rh_d2_modular_scattering/figures/`.

---

## 1. The object — the 600-cell / icosian ring
**Math:** the regular 4-polytope with 120 vertices = the unit icosians (binary
icosahedral group $2I$); the lattice is a copy of $E_8$; the order whose theta series
realizes $\zeta_K(s)\zeta_K(s-1)$ over $\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt5)$.
**Prompt:** *"Elegant mathematical art of the 600-cell, a four-dimensional regular
polytope shown as a symmetric 3D projection: 120 luminous golden vertices joined by a
dense intricate web of thin glowing edges, nested icosahedral and dodecahedral shells,
crystalline and perfectly symmetric, deep dark navy background, a precise glowing
geometric object, scientific illustration."*

## 2. The bell — the modular cavity
**Math:** the modular surface $\mathbb{H}^2/\mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{Z})$ — a hyperbolic
"bell" with one cusp running to infinity; the zeros of $\zeta$ appear as scattering
resonances of this leaky cavity.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual scientific art of a hyperbolic bell resonator: an infinite
curved trumpet/funnel (a pseudosphere cusp) with a thin glowing neck stretching up to
infinity, opening into a wide curved bowl, faint concentric standing-wave ripples
resonating inside, dark cosmic background, deep teal and gold light, a ringing cavity."*

## 3. The mirror — the functional equation
**Math:** $\xi(s)=\xi(1-s)$ — reflection across the critical line $\mathrm{Re}(s)=1/2$;
the geometric inversion $z\mapsto -1/z$ folds the cavity onto itself; on the line the
scattering is lossless, $|\varphi|=1$.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual art of a perfect mirror symmetry: a luminous vertical axis
down the centre, with a glowing curved cavity on the left reflected exactly onto the
right, light folding across the central line, deep indigo and silver, a sense of a
self-dual reflection, mathematically precise and serene."*

## 4. The spectrum — zeros as a critical-edge gas
**Math:** the nontrivial zeros lie (conjecturally) on $\mathrm{Re}(s)=1/2$; their
statistics are GUE ($\beta=2$, level repulsion); as a de Bruijn–Newman flow they sit
at the critical edge $\Lambda=0$, a repelling "Dyson gas" poised at criticality.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual scientific art: a single vertical luminous straight line
against a dark background, a row of brilliant points strung along it like glowing beads
that repel their neighbours (faint force-fields between them), the whole row balanced on
a knife-edge, electric blue and white on one perfect axis, ordered chaos at a critical
balance."*

## 5. The loop — the zeros spell out the primes
**Math:** the explicit formula — primes $\leftrightarrow$ zeros are a self-referential
loop; summed as oscillations $\sum_n\cos(\gamma_n u)$, the zeros peak exactly at
$u=\log(\text{prime}^k)$.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual art of a feedback loop between two glowing rings: one ring
labelled with prime numbers (2,3,5,7,11), the other with a wave/oscillation pattern,
arrows circling between them, light flowing from the wave-ring to spikes that land on
the primes, gold and cyan, a sense of two things generating each other, elegant."*

## 6. The wall and the void — the missing object
**Math:** RH = one positivity $W(f)\ge 0$; geometry reaches the cuspidal side ($\zeta_K$)
but not $\zeta$; the missing object is a canonical Frobenius over $\mathbb{Z}$ whose
degree-form is that positivity — its absence is a precise empty silhouette.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual art of a missing object: bright reachable geometric structures
(golden polytopes, spheres, lattices) crowding up to a sharp luminous vertical wall,
beyond which lies a precise empty silhouette — a negative-space outline of an unknown
form, dark and unfilled, just out of reach; known geometry glows gold, the void is deep
violet-black; a frontier between the built and the unbuilt."* (wide / 16:9)

## 7. The two worlds — what the substrate reaches
**Math:** the substrate realizes $\zeta_K=\zeta\cdot L(\chi_5)$; the 24-cell (no
$\sqrt5$) carries $\zeta$'s zeros, the $\varphi$-shell carries $L(\chi_5)$; $\zeta$ is
contained as a factor but not isolated — the $(O2)$ negative.
**Prompt:** *"Conceptual art of one crystalline object splitting into two streams of
light: a central glowing polytope from which a golden stream (labelled by Riemann
zeros) and a violet stream (a second L-function) diverge, the two never fully separating
back into the single object; dark background, a sense of one thing containing two."*

- Add *"clean, minimal, no text labels, scientific poster style, dark background"* if
  you want them to read as figures.
- Ask for *16:9* for 2, 4, 6 (landscape); *1:1* for 1, 3, 5, 7.
- These are **metaphors**, not proofs — keep that framing if you ever caption them.
