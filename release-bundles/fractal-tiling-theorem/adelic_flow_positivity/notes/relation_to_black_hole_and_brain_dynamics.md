# Relation to black-hole / brain dynamics (interpretation only)

The transferable mathematical structure is **contraction / dissipative semigroup**, not
"flow orientation":
- black-hole ringdown: damped QNMs = a **dissipative** (non-unitary) evolution; positivity
  of the greybody/flux comes from the absorbing boundary, not from time's arrow.
- brain attractor dynamics: a **Lyapunov / contraction** to a basin, not reversible flow.
- adelic centre: positivity = the **capacity contraction** `‖K‖≤1`, generated by the PSD
  archimedean+pole capacity.
In all three the positive ingredient is a **contraction/dissipation**, and a purely
reversible (unitary) flow contributes none of it. No analogy counts as proof.
