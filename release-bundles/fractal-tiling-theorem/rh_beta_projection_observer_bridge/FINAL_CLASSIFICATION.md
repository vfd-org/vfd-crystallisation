## Answer to the WO's boxed question

> Is the β=4→β=2 mismatch a fatal obstruction, a projection artefact, or the real
> observer-compatibility bridge?

**None of the three cleanly — and that is the result.** Precisely:

- It is **not fatal**: a canonical, non-circular projection β=4→β=2 exists
  (complexification `ℍ⊗_ℝℂ ≅ M₂(ℂ)`, `2I ⊂ SU(2)`), preserving self-adjointness,
  positivity, and trace.
- It is **partly a projection artefact / category error**: the icosian substrate
  is β=4 as a *symmetry class*, not as *spacing* (9 distinct eigenvalues among
  120). The zeros' β=2 is a *spacing* fact. "GSE-spacing → GUE-spacing" is not the
  real comparison; no canonical spacing projection exists.
- It is **the observer bridge only in the RH-equivalent sense**: the sole map
  that reaches the zeros is the explicit formula / Weil pairing, i.e. Weil
  positivity — the known wall.

## Which WO successes were achieved

- **Success A (obstruction clarified):** the β-mismatch is not fatal and not a
  single spacing transition; it is an RH-free complexification plus the Weil wall.
- **Success C (candidate bridge object):** complexification is a non-circular
  canonical β=4→β=2 projection preserving arithmetic structure.
- **Success D (reduction to Weil positivity):** everything RH-relevant in the

Not achieved: Success B as hoped (no projection moves *spacing* β=4→β=2
non-circularly) — and no proof (Grade 3/4).
