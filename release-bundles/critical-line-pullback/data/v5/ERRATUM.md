# Erratum (v1.1, 2026-06-12)

Logs/findings in this directory were generated by the v1 runs and may use
v1's over-strong language (e.g. "THEOREM", "universality", wording locating zeros on the block, "tau identified"). The v1.1 paper revision and the corrected
sim sources supersede that wording: Finding 5 is a bookkeeping-level
candidate for tau (no operator-level construction); Finding 7 is a
conditional suppression lemma (not universality); zero attributions are
scalar-factor level only. Numeric CSV/log content is unchanged and remains
valid as data.
