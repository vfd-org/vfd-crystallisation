# Extension IV — Withheld

This folder is for Extension IV, *Cosmic Self-Pruning and Frame Recurrence*.

## Why it is withheld

Extension IV is a speculative structural extension of the programme. It is
**not load-bearing** for any of the primary review-packet claims:

- Paper I (Existence as Closure) does not depend on it.
- Paper II (Life as Closure) does not depend on it.
- Note C (Closure-as-Distance) does not depend on it.
- Note B (Cortical Phase Closure) does not depend on it.
- Paper III (From Processing to Point of View) does not depend on it.
- Notes A, D, E, F do not depend on it.

The cascade-rung-tower demonstration originally developed within Extension
IV's `cosmic_demo.py` is included in Paper I's repro folder as
`papers/I-existence-as-closure/repro/rung_tower_demo.py`, with an explicit
standalone-artifact note. No recurrence or cosmic-pruning claims from
Extension IV are used in Paper I; only the per-rung substrate /
closure-operator / spectral-$\tau$ construction.

## Status

Extension IV remains a draft. Its substantive math content (the
non-periodic recurrence Conditional Proposition under H-aperiodic, the
pruning operator, the frame-destruction conditions) has been demoted to
the appropriate Conditional Proposition status in its current source. It
is not part of v1.0.0-rc1.

## Why this matters for review

A common failure mode in research programmes is to use speculative
extensions as rhetorical support for primary claims. The deliberate
separation here is intended to prevent that. If you encounter any
sentence in the primary packet that depends rhetorically on cosmic
recurrence, universe-as-frame, or frame-pruning material, please flag it
as a scope-discipline issue.
