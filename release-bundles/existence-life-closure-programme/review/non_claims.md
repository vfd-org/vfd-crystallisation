# What this programme does not claim

A consolidated list of non-claims, distilled from each paper's
"What this paper does not claim" or equivalent scope sections.

## The programme does not claim

1. **Consciousness is proven.** The Access Principle (P-A) is named as a conjectural hypothesis. The framework provides candidate structural conditions for owned experience; it does not assert that any system has owned experience.
2. **Empirical proxies directly measure the formal closure operator.** Closure-as-distance, $R_{\mathrm{cl}}$, $R_{\mathrm{phase}}$, $\widehat{\delta}_{AB}^{\mathrm{EEG}}$, $\mathrm{FlowIndex}$, $\mathrm{CCR}$ are all first-order proxies, not the operator itself.
3. **The cosmic-extension material is required for the primary results.** Extension IV (Cosmic Self-Pruning and Frame Recurrence) is withheld from the primary review packet. No primary claim depends on its content.
4. **Suffering is reduced to a number.** Dyscoherence $\mathcal{D}_\Ical$ is a structural proxy for *the condition under which* pain, distress, disorientation, or trauma may become likely under P-A. It is not pain itself.
5. **Specific qualitative character is identified by spectral signatures (P-A alone).** The qualia identification of Paper II requires both P-A and the additional P-A-spectral hypothesis. Neither is proven.
6. **The hard problem of consciousness is solved.** The framework supplies a structural correlate that would correlate with qualitative variation if P-A and P-A-spectral hold; it does not explain why qualitative character exists.
7. **ARIA-chess is conscious.** Paper III is explicit: ARIA-chess satisfies the structural conditions for a bounded reference frame; whether it has owned experience requires P-A and is not asserted.
8. **The CAD-D1–D5-v1 diagnostic thresholds are universal.** They are empirical thresholds derived from the current substrate set, labelled CAD-D1–D5-v1. CAD-v1.5 recalibration on larger cohort sets is open work.
9. **A confirmed empirical PASS exists on all multi-channel integrative substrates.** Only source-localised propofol-anaesthesia EEG (Note B) is a strict-CAD-PASS empirical anchor. The trait-anxiety substrate (Note D) is PASS-leaning. The FlowIndex and joint-meaning proxies have no real-data PASS yet.
10. **Future pre-registered tests are current evidence.** PTSD pre/post-therapy EEG, wet-lab planarian DiBAC4(3), TMS-EEG, conversational hyperscanning, music-performance flow EEG — these are committed pre-registered tests, not current data.
11. **Theorems are proven beyond their stated assumptions.** Every theorem and conditional proposition states its hypothesis stack. Removing the hypotheses removes the conclusion.
12. **The framework is unfalsifiable.** Every claim has a falsifier; see [`../01-falsifiers-and-roadmap.md`](../01-falsifiers-and-roadmap.md).
13. **The biological substrate is settled.** Hypotheses H-RP-1/2/3 (rung–cortical, predictive-coding, bio-substrate) name the open questions about substrate mapping. They are conjectural.
14. **Cosmic recurrence is established.** Even within the withheld Extension IV, the non-periodic recurrence claim is a Conditional Proposition under H-aperiodic, not a theorem.

## Reviewer escape hatch

If you spot any place in any paper where the prose appears to make a claim
in this non-claim list, please open a GitHub issue tagged "scope". Such
issues are treated as substantive review feedback.
