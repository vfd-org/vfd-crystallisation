# Existence / Life / Closure Programme — Review Packet Overview

*A bounded-reference-frame approach to existence, living systems, and measurable integrative organisation.*
*Review packet prepared 2026-05-22.*

## 1. Programme thesis

This programme proposes closure as a structural condition for existence,
life, and point-of-view. The foundation paper defines bounded reference
frames and per-frame zero-lines. The life paper extends the same
machinery into boundary, repair, memory, agency, relevance, dyscoherence,
purpose, and joint meaning. The empirical notes do not claim to measure
the formal operator directly; they develop first-order proxies,
especially closure-as-distance, with explicit diagnostics for when those
proxies are applicable. The consciousness paper is a conditional bridge,
not a proof of phenomenology.

1. **Internal mathematical coherence** of Papers I–III: are the structural definitions and conditional propositions internally consistent under the stated $\tau$ convention?
2. **Status-label discipline**: theorem vs conditional proposition vs empirical proxy vs pre-registered proposal — is the boundary kept clean throughout?

## 5. Conventions in the packet

- **Theorem / Definition** = formal structural claim under stated assumptions.
- **Conditional Proposition** = bridge claim depending on H-RP, P-A, substrate mapping, or empirical interpretation.
- **Empirical Result** = completed data analysis with stated effect size + significance.
- **Empirical Proxy** = measurable first-order approximation, not the formal operator.
- **Pre-registered Proposal** = future test, not evidence.

Two $\tau$ conventions appear in Paper I and downstream:

- $\tau_{\mathrm{ico}}$ (icosian / Galois): $\dim \mathrm{Fix}(\tau_{\mathrm{ico}}) = 94$, used in theorem statements.
- $\tau_{\mathrm{spec}}$ (spectral / basis-robust): $\dim \mathrm{Fix}(\tau_{\mathrm{spec}}) = 116$, used in sim demos.

Both are involutions and commute with $C_\varphi$; dimension-specific claims state which $\tau$ is used.

The programme commits to the following falsifications:

- If the closure-as-distance proxy under CAD-D1–D5-v1 fails to replicate on any independent open EEG anaesthesia cohort with comparable diagnostic profile, the methodology is wrong.
