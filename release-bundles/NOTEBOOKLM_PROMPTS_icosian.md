## AUDIO OVERVIEW (Deep Dive) — customization prompt

Two hosts, ~12 min, for a curious but skeptical listener who knows what the Riemann
Hypothesis is but isn't a specialist. Tell the story of ONE geometric object — the
icosian "closure" structure on the 600-cell over Q(sqrt5) — and what arithmetic it
secretly carries.

Arc: (1) the object and why a shape would have arithmetic at all; (2) its EXACT shadow
L(Theta,s)=zeta_K(s)*zeta_K(s-1) — the Riemann zeta sits inside as ONE factor of four;
(3) the honest twist: the authors then PROVE the object does NOT single out zeta — a
deliberate negative result; (4) the witness — the object's "cuspidal face" hands you the
prime side of a Weil positivity test for free; (5) name the wall plainly and stop there.

Non-negotiable framing — repeat in your own words: This is NOT a proof of the Riemann
Hypothesis and the hosts must never say it "solves/cracks/proves" RH. The one open step
is GRH for a single cuspidal L-function — not classical RH. The exact identity is carried
by a classical theorem (Siegel-Weil); the computer just checks the implementation. The
numerical positivity tests are small and truncated — they validate the code, not the
universal claim. Do NOT connect this to cosmology or physics — that's a separate
interpretive frame the authors explicitly firewall off.

Tone: warm, precise, genuinely impressed by the HONESTY of the work — the hook is "here's
a result that tells you exactly where its own wall is," not hype. Let one host play the
skeptic who keeps asking "but does that prove RH?" and the other correct them: "no — and
that's the point."

## VIDEO OVERVIEW — customization prompt

A ~5-7 min visual explainer for a general technical audience. Build it around ONE image:
a single object with two faces. Walk the viewer from the 600-cell / 120 unit icosians ->
the maximal order over Q(sqrt5) -> its theta series -> the exact identity
L(Theta,s)=zeta_K(s)*zeta_K(s-1), C_2=1. Show zeta as ONE of four factors, then show the
"gate" rejecting the stronger claim that the object IS zeta (degree 4 != 1). Close on the
witness: the cuspidal face -> prime side of a Weil positivity witness -> the single named
wall.

Visual emphasis: the factorisation as a labelled product; a clear "one object, two
arithmetic faces (Eisenstein / cuspidal)" diagram; a status badge on each claim —
VERIFIED / RECALLED / INTERPRETATION.

Hard rules: Headline on screen and in narration must include "Not a proof of the Riemann
Hypothesis." The open step is GRH for one cuspidal L-function, not classical RH. Never
imply RH is solved. Keep cosmology/physics out entirely — geometry-first only. End on the
honest note: the contribution is a clean map of the boundary and where the wall is, not a
crossing of it.
