The Picture of the Primes

Five scenes: a local mechanism that is completely understood and verified to a fraction of a percent, a family of open global questions — each a statement about the zeros of an explicit L-function — the exact dictionary between the two layers, and the place where the open half becomes a question about geometry. Every figure is computed; every claim has a falsifier.

4The crystal question interactive

A periodic crystal diffracts into sharp peaks. A golden-ratio Fibonacci chain diffracts into sharp peaks with no period — a quasicrystal. The primes, read as waves, diffract into peaks that land on the zeros of the zeta function — every zero in the plotted range aligns with a local peak. Dyson's proposal: under the Riemann Hypothesis the zeros form a one-dimensional quasicrystal in a precise generalized sense, so classifying 1-D quasicrystals could prove RH. The classification is open; stated, not claimed.
