## THE ONE-LINE STORY (use verbatim or close to it)

A single geometric object — the maximal icosian order on the 600-cell over ℚ(√5) — has an
exact arithmetic shadow, L(Θ,s) = ζ_K(s)·ζ_K(s−1). The Riemann ζ sits inside it as one
factor among four, but the object provably does NOT isolate ζ. Its cuspidal face supplies
the parameter-free prime side of a Weil positivity witness, and the one step that remains
open is named precisely: GRH for a single cuspidal L-function — not classical RH.

## HARD FRAMING RULES (do not violate)

1. NEVER state or imply that RH or any GRH is proved, or that this is "progress toward
   proving RH." The honest verb is "locates / certifies / reaches the wall."
2. Geometry-first. The mathematics stands on its own; VFD physical/cosmological language is
   FRAME, not proof. Present any physical reading only in a clearly-labelled
   "Interpretation (non-load-bearing)" box.
3. COSMOLOGY AND RH NEVER TOUCH AS A CLAIM. The note-shared-object firewall is the rule:
   one geometric object can appear in multiple roles with NO proven cross-role implication.
   Never write "the universe encodes the primes" or anything that reads that way.
4. Every headline claim gets a status stamp: VERIFIED (computed/proved here),
   RECALLED (classical theorem imported), or INTERPRETATION (non-load-bearing).
5. The exact identity (C₂ = 1) is carried by the classical Siegel–Weil / maximal-order
   theorem; the computation verifies the implementation. Say it that way — don't claim the
   computation alone proves an infinite Euler product.
6. The finite positivity tests are archimedean-dominated and truncated (N(𝔮) ≤ 150, no
   tail bound): they validate the implementation, NOT the universal quantifier. If you
   mention the W ≥ 0 results, mention this in the same breath.
