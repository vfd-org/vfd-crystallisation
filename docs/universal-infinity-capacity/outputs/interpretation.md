# Interpretation & verdict

**WEAK PASS.** RH and black-hole instantiations are **concrete** (they reuse the verified
Weil/Connes algebra and the scattering flux identity); brain and generic-VFD are
**conceptual analogies only**. The unifying object `ICG=(X,∂X,Φ,H,R,Q,K,L)` and the
stability law `Q≥0 ⟺ ‖K‖≤1` are coherent and reusable — but the unification is **generic**
(the algebra holds for any PSD `H`+sym `R`), so it is an **organizing language**, not new
mathematics and not a proof in any domain.

Useful deliverables: the portable definition, the domain table, the five diagnostics, and
the RH-instance numbers. Honest limit: "infinity = completed boundary with capacity" is a
good *reframing*; it does not move any domain's open theorem. For RH specifically it
re-states the same wall (infinite `‖K‖≤1` = Weil positivity = arithmetic site).

> Infinity is the completed boundary of a flow; a stable infinity is one whose capacity
> dominates reflection; failure at infinity is leakage. True, reusable, and shallow — a
> language, not a proof.
