**D. Must-Fix Issues**
1. **Combable/roomy contradiction remains in Chapter 11.**  
Ch5’s corrected box says circle, 3-sphere, 7-sphere, with latter two roomy ([333](/tmp/book-for-review.md:333)). But Ch11 says our 3D space is “the only one of the three roomy enough” ([596](/tmp/book-for-review.md:596)), then immediately says the 7-sphere is “one other roomy option” ([600](/tmp/book-for-review.md:600)).  
Fix: “Our three-dimensional space is one of the two roomy options; the circle is too cramped.”

2. **“Combed at all” is still imprecise.**  
At [333](/tmp/book-for-review.md:333), change “spheres you can comb at all” to “spheres you can comb completely” or “parallelizable spheres.” Ordinary hairy-ball “combable” can mean one nonzero tangent field; the book means a full frame.

3. **Ledger gauge row has a confusing extra dimension.**  
The Standard Model row says “three symmetry groups” but lists “dims 14/8/3/1” ([812](/tmp/book-for-review.md:812)). A physicist will ask why 14 is there.  
Fix: “parent G2 dim 14; SM gauge skeleton SU(3), SU(2), U(1) dims 8/3/1.”

4. **E8 date wording is now stale.**  
“Proven in full this past decade” ([198](/tmp/book-for-review.md:198)) is borderline false in June 2026; Viazovska’s E8 proof was submitted in March 2016 and published in 2017.  
Fix: “proved in 2016/2017.”

5. **B-anomaly wording is basically fixed, but harden one phrase.**  
The manuscript correctly distinguishes `$C_9$` from `$R_K$` ([457](/tmp/book-for-review.md:457), [819](/tmp/book-for-review.md:819)). Replace “genuine anomaly” at [463](/tmp/book-for-review.md:463) with “persisting angular tension,” and optionally add “subject to hadronic-theory uncertainties.” That prevents a skeptical flavour physicist from objecting before reaching the tie language.
