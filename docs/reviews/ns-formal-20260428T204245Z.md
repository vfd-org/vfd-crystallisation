**1. Claim Audit**

- Main proposition, `:142-160`: not proved as stated. The proof uses `Cascade-NS correspondence` (`:367-414`) and `B_NS` (`:506-518`) as if they supply the missing continuum bridge, but both are open/unsupported at the needed strength. The “unique smooth global solution on `R^3`” conclusion is only a tautological consequence if the bridge already gives uniform `L^\infty` vorticity for the actual NS solution.
- `\Pi_{\mathrm{hyd}}` lemma, `:162-195`: not established. The scale sum is wrong as written: `\sum_k \varphi^{-2k}` over `k in Z` diverges (`:193`); only `k >= 0` gives `1+\varphi` (`:250`). Also vorticity is one derivative of velocity, not “two derivatives” (`:202-204`), unless a different operator is being used and defined.
- F2 import, `:315-321`: overstates Paper XXXVI. That paper classifies order-`<=2`, degree-`<=2` symmetric-tensor scalar densities under H-MIN; it does not prove “no rank-3 or higher `W(E8)`-invariant local scalar invariants” in general.
- F4, `:323-334`: source-consistent. Paper XXXVI supports `dim V = 583` as a graded closure-field dimension count, not a spectral theorem.
- F5, `:336-346`: partially source-consistent, but “up to a factor of 2” blurs two different things: per-copy equality under H-SIG versus the factor-2 residue/additivity mechanism, which Paper XXXVI treats separately.
- Cascade-NS correspondence theorem, `:367-415`: not proved. The “proof” is schematic coefficient matching; it does not derive NS from the closure gradient flow. P7 only gives a conditional weak distributional `S^3` limit under a scaling/compactness package, not this theorem.
- Rank preservation lemma, `:429-461`: not proved. Linear coarse-graining preserving tensor rank does not imply absence or boundedness of vortex stretching. The proof concedes vortex stretching is rank-2 (`:458-460`), so the stated conclusion does not follow.
- Bounded macroscopic operators lemma, `:465-500`: overclaims. Item (3), “higher Sobolev norms are bounded for all `t >= 0`” (`:472`), is essentially global regularity. The proof reduces to the still-open `L^\infty` vorticity bound (`:495-500`).
- `B_NS` hypothesis, `:506-518`: acceptable as a hypothesis, but “smooth Leray--Hopf solution” (`:507`) mixes weak and smooth solution classes.
- BKM theorem/corollary, `:561-590`: the conditional logic is fine once a uniform vorticity bound is assumed. The citation should not rely only on the Euler BKM paper for an NS continuation criterion.
- Numerical claims: the 600-cell spectrum and `78.3%/21.7%` split (`:636-651`) are locally supported by the scripts. The viscosity estimate `10^{-6} m²/s` (`:417-423`) is not derived. The aria H4O Lyapunov/torus claims (`:252-261`, `:689-695`) are not locally verifiable; the cited `aria-chess/...` paths are absent.
