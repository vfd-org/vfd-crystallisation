**2. Internal Consistency**

The central inconsistency is that `H_OS-lift` is declared open at `101-115`, `238-242`, `1286-1298`, but Sections 3.4–6 then present continuum measure existence, OS axioms, and mass gap as proved (`513-842`, `1101-1148`).

F4 is simultaneously “dimension count only” (`372-382`) and a spectral/operator bound (`132-134`, `149-150`, `567-591`, `837-841`). That is a direct contradiction.

The gauge-group paragraph is wrong: `G_2` is already compact; `SU(3)` is the stabilizer of a unit imaginary octonion, not “the maximal compact subgroup of `G_2`” (`427-431`). It also conflicts with the quaternion-subalgebra wording.

All `\ref` targets appear defined by static scan, but several are semantically wrong: `Definition~\ref{hyp:OS-lift}` refers to a `hypothesis`; `Theorem~\ref{thm:main}` refers to a proposition; `Theorem~\ref{thm:F2/F5/F6}` often refers to hypothesis environments.

**3. External Consistency**

Paper XXXVI verifies the scoped status of F2/F4/F5/F6/F7. The YM manuscript’s scoped statements mostly match it, but the body later uses stronger obsolete forms.
