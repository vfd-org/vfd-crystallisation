**1. Claim Audit**
- L155: “Restricted-model separation” is not proved. It depends on Theorem L290, whose proof is only an informal state-counting argument and would imply a lower bound of P-vs-NP strength. This must be a bridge axiom or conjecture, not a theorem.
- L167: “Conditional P vs NP” is not established. The proof silently needs H$_{\sigma\text{-irr-NP}}$, the invalid L290 theorem, unproved absence of all other structures, and theorem-grade CTM/TM transport.
- L226: CT equivalence is imported, but local sources only support this as framework/conditional material, not Clay-grade theorem.
- L248: Generic σ-irreducibility is false as stated: the theorem says any $\alpha>0$, while the proof only gives decay for $\alpha>1$. The per-clause $1/2$ bound is asserted, not proved.
- L280: Corollary inherits the defects of L248.
- L290: σ-P limitation is the central overclaim. “Exponential state-space” plus “polynomial access” is not a lower bound. No query model, indistinguishability lemma, adversary argument, or reduction is given.
- L332: The claim that σ-reducibility is in NP is not proved. Verifying the “for all assignments” condition appears exponential unless “equals” is syntactic and formalized very narrowly.
- L361: “At least as hard as NP=coNP” is not justified. A single coNP language being in NP does not imply coNP $\subseteq$ NP without completeness.
- L374: Conditional NP-containment is tautological and acceptable.
- L384: Separation corollary fails because L290 fails; it also omits an explicit proof that $\mathrm{P}_\sigma^{cas}\subseteq\mathrm{NP}^{cas}$.
- L437: Structural completeness is acceptable only as a named bridge conjecture. The “Equivalently” clause is not formal.
- L474: The decomposition into c1–c5 is not an equality. c1–c4 are existence claims; the conjecture needs necessity/exhaustion.
- L513, L533, L556: Barrier-evasion propositions are not proved. “Uses a non-oracle property,” “syntactic not semantic,” and “specific Galois extension” do not establish non-relativization, non-naturalness, or non-algebrization.
- L575: Theorem “Cascade evades three barriers” is unsupported and should be removed or demoted to a scope remark.
- L599–626: The 94/120 and 26/120 spectral counts match the local script output. But the script uses numerical eigendecomposition and heuristic recognition; it is not an exact proof of the closed-form spectrum. No bridge to H$_{\sigma\text{-irr-NP}}$ is established.
- L630–649: The R2/R3 “1.0000” diagnostic is imposed by an explicit closure projector in the script, not discovered. It is not independent evidence for the bridge.
- L653–659: The aria artifact is not present at the cited local paths, so I could not verify it.
- L687–700: “What is proved” overstates the situation. L290, L248, and L575 are not proved.
- L740–741: “If Conjecture can be proved, we have a proof of P≠NP” is incomplete and false as written; H$_{\sigma\text{-irr-NP}}$, a real lower-bound theorem, and CT transport are also needed.
