**1. Claim Audit**

1. `pnp-formal.tex:155`: “Under Hσ-irr-NP and Bσ-P, Pσ^cas ⊊ NP^cas.”  
As stated, this is only a tautological conditional: H gives NP containment and B gives non-membership in Pσ. But the proof section does not match it. `pnp-formal.tex:384` states the corollary under Hσ-irr-NP alone, while `pnp-formal.tex:392` uses `thm:sigmaPlim`, which is actually the open hypothesis Bσ-P. This is still a missing hypothesis.

2. `pnp-formal.tex:174`: “Under Hσ-irr-NP, Bσ-P, and Conjecture 6.1, P != NP.”  
The proof at `pnp-formal.tex:483-507` assumes only Conjecture 6.1 at line 484, then uses Bσ-P at line 490 and Hσ-irr-NP at line 502 without assuming either. It also asserts at `pnp-formal.tex:494-497` that generic σ-irreducible 3-SAT lacks rung-localization, φ-adic, and 2I structure. No definitions or proof are supplied for those predicates on SAT instances.

3. `pnp-formal.tex:257`: “For any α > 0, probability of σ-reducibility is O(2^{-cn}).”  
The proof only derives decay for α > 1 at `pnp-formal.tex:283-284`. The fixed-partition estimate at `pnp-formal.tex:271-275` is asserted, not proved, and does not follow from the definition. The theorem is not established.

4. `pnp-formal.tex:345-351`: “Lσ-red is in NP ... verifying P takes polynomial time.”  
Not established. Under the definition at `pnp-formal.tex:246-250`, verification appears to require checking all orbit-constant assignments on A unless a syntactic certificate/equality criterion is supplied. The paper gives neither.

5. `pnp-formal.tex:513-579`: barrier-evasion propositions.  
These do not prove what they claim. `pnp-formal.tex:523-526` says the separation survives relativization by invoking Bσ-P as if it were a theorem. `pnp-formal.tex:556-570` misstates algebrization: using one named Galois involution is not enough to show a proof is non-algebrizing in the Aaronson-Wigderson sense.
