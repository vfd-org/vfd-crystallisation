- Corollary 3.5 (375-385): not proved as stated; it omits `B_{\sigma-P}`, used at 383.
- Conjecture 4.1 (428-432): fine as a conjecture, but “equivalently” is not meaningful until “exploits” and “structural property” are formalized.
- Conditional P vs NP proof (474-497): fails independently at 484-488. “σ-irreducible instances have NO σ-symmetry” is false as stated; irreducibility is not the same as non-invariance. The claimed absence of rung-localization, φ-adic, and `2I` structure for generic SAT is asserted without theorem.
