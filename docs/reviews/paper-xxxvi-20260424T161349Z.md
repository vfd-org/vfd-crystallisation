**Claim Audit**
Round 4 did not fully close the Round 3 defects.

- **F1, “unique positive solution ... globally attracting”** (L156-L165): established. The proof is adequate.

- **F2 proposition, “space ... is three-dimensional and spanned by R,E,Q”** (L219-L226): not established. The proof discards trace-square and trace-kinetic terms by invoking a “minimality-of-kinetic-content gauge choice” supposedly in Definition 2, but Definition 2(iv) is only “Scalar-valued” (L213). The invariant classification is therefore unsupported. Consequently **F2** (L265-L270) is not proved.

- **H-NC / F3**: H-NC is now explicit (L306-L317), so the old crystallographic-restriction error is no longer the stated mechanism in the paper body. But **F3** still states uniqueness under only the two axioms plus conditions (a),(c) (L341-L346), omitting H-NC. The proof also says the `E8 -> H4` step is “forced by F1 ... and Axiom E8, not imposed” (L352), contradicting the honest scope remark at L375-L377. F3 remains overclaimed.

- **Old `8_v+8_s+8_c` error**: the specific root-decomposition error is fixed at L362. However, an old Spin(8) relic remains: “excluded by the existence of the `35_v` representation at D4” (L713). That is inconsistent with the new SO(4) formulation.

- **F4, “closure depth ... 583”** (L422-L431): the arithmetic follows from Definition 4, but the definition encodes the result. The deeper claim that only D4 carries `R^24 \otimes R^24` is not established and is later justified by F7 (L443), making F4 dependent on the disputed F7 argument.

- **F5 lemma/theorem** (L508-L536): the finite-sum equality is now logically valid under H-SIG, assuming the discrete difference operators are genuinely sigma-compatible. This fixes the Round 3 equality/conjugacy problem at the discrete level. But the continuum carryover (L536, L544) is not proved by GH convergence alone. Also, the abstract and summary omit H-SIG (L63, L864, L840).

- **F6, GH convergence with rate** (L583-L589): not established. The proof admits the rate is conditional on H-HR (L598, L605-L610), but the theorem states it unconditionally. The claimed unconditional density via Kuranishi (L598) is not shown; finite `2I/2T` coset shifts do not by themselves give a dense subgroup of SO(4). BI2 is also only sketched.

- **F7 SO(4) replacement**: the representation identity `Sym^2(R^4)=1+9_TS` (L652-L657) is correct. The conclusion is not. A 9-dimensional symmetric traceless SO(4) representation is not, by itself, a Fierz-Pauli massless spin-2 field; the latter needs Lorentzian structure, gauge redundancy/constraints, and a field equation. The exclusions of H4 and 16 (L697-L699) are asserted, not proved. Triality distinguishes D4, but does not prove unique physical localisation of Lambda.

- **F8 coefficient determination** (L762-L769): the algebraic matching is fine as matching. It is not a derivation. The paper itself admits the Maxwell and SU(2) rung assignments are structural hypotheses (L790-L795), so “cascade-determined” and “none is a free parameter” are too strong.

- **Zero-Parameter Corollary** (L776-L778): false as stated. The proof immediately weakens it to “beyond the three imported couplings” (L781).

- **Cascade Lambda Theorem** (L805-L810): not proved. It omits H-NC, H-SIG, H-FP, and the unproved shell-amplitude/product/residue-to-Lambda assumptions. The numerical value is correct, but the derivation is not theorem-grade.
