**1. Claim Audit**
- **F1 is the only clean theorem here.** Claim: “the self-similarity equation … has a unique positive solution … globally attracting” ([paper-xxxvi.tex](/mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:127)). The proof at lines 141–156 does establish the fixed point and attraction on `(0,\infty)`. The only weakness is that the “straightforward check” for entry into `J=[3/2,2]` is omitted, but the argument is salvageable.
- **F2 Proposition over-claims badly.** Claim: “the space of admissible scalar densities is at most three-dimensional” ([...:190](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:190>)). The proof silently imports gauge fixing and on-shell reduction from F7 at lines 205 and 209, neither present in Definition 2 nor in the proposition statement. It also ignores obvious quadratic invariants for a symmetric 2-tensor, e.g. `(tr Φ)^2`, `∇_ρΦ_{μν}∇^ρΦ^{μν}`, `∇_μ trΦ ∇^μ trΦ`, and mixed second-derivative contractions. The stated classification is not proved.
- **F2 theorem therefore fails.** Claim: “Any closure functional … has the form … `αR+βE−γQ`” ([...:218](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:218>)). Since Proposition F2-basis is unsupported, the theorem is unsupported. As written it is false without extra hypotheses.
- **F3 is not proved; it is a modelling choice disguised as uniqueness.** Claim: “the preferred maximal descending chain … is … `E8→H4→40→D4→16→8→0` … no intermediate rung exists” ([...:262](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:262>)). The proof simply restates condition (b), then introduces non-Coxeter objects `40,16,8,0`, and asserts uniqueness at lines 289–290 without classification. The theorem mixes root systems and ad hoc polytopal data, so the conclusion does not follow from the stated premises.
- **F4 proves only arithmetic on a definition, not a physically justified depth.** Claim: “`N_total=583` … seven independent scalar-boundary shells plus `24^2` independent rank-2 tensor components” ([...:332](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:332>)). The proof at lines 344–346 is correct for Definition 4.1. But the definition itself is loaded: the `24^2` term includes the antisymmetric summand `Λ^2(R^24)` at lines 314–316, inconsistent with the paper’s repeated insistence that the relevant field is symmetric rank-2. So the calculation is internally chosen, not derived.
- **The shell amplitude `K=φ^{-1}` is asserted, not derived.** Claim at lines 321–325 ([...:321](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:321>)). Nothing in F1 or F2 implies that “the leading non-trivial power is `φ^{-1}`.” This unsupported step is then used in the `Λ` theorem.
- **F5 is partly circular and does not support the factor-2 corollary.** Claim: “`F` is a `σ`-intertwiner” ([...:374](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:374>)). But Definition 2 already requires invariance under `σ` at line 182. More seriously, the corollary claim “`F|_{E8}=F|_{H4}+F|_{H4'}`” ([...:390](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:390>)) is never made meaningful, because `F` was defined as an integral over the continuum lift `\overline E`, not a sum over a decomposition of the discrete root set.
- **F6 is not established.** Claim: “Schläfli refinement GH-converges to `S^3`, with covering-radius rate `ε_n≤ε_0 2^{-n}`” ([...:434](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:434>)). The proof explicitly says the halving rule is “assumed” at line 445, then concludes both BI1 and BI2 are “established” at line 449. That is not acceptable. The density claim via Kuranishi is also merely asserted.
- **F7 is only a conditional restatement of its own hypotheses, not a proof of localisation.** Claim: “the closure contribution carrying `Λ` localises at the `D4` rung” ([...:493](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:493>)). The first half follows conditionally from Deser if one grants a Fierz-Pauli field. The localisation step at lines 500–501 is rhetoric, not deduction.
- **F8 does not match the actions it names.** Claim: coefficients are “fixed by action-normalisation matching” ([...:557](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:557>)). But `Q[Φ]` is a quadratic symmetric-tensor invariant, not Maxwell `F_{μν}F^{μν}` on an antisymmetric field; `E[Φ]` is divergence-squared, not Yang-Mills kinetic term. The match is type-mismatched before numbers enter.
- **The zero-parameter corollary collapses with F2/F4/F8.** Claim at lines 570–576 ([...:570](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:570>)). Not proved.
- **The `Λ` theorem is not established.** Claim: “`Λ l_P^2 = 2 φ^{-583}`” ([...:594](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:594>)). The arithmetic is fine once you assume `φ^{-1}`, `583`, the factor `2`, and the identification with `Λ`. The proof establishes none of those nontrivial identifications.

**2. Internal Consistency**
- Definition 2 requires `σ`-invariance of the density already ([...:182](</mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxxvi/paper-xxxvi.tex:182>)), so F5 cannot then be presented as a new theorem proving `σ`-intertwining.
- The paper oscillates between **symmetric rank-2 fields** (lines 174, 190, 493) and the full `24×24` tensor space including `Λ^2(R^24)` (lines 314–316). That directly contaminates the `583` count.
- F2 works on a continuum field over `\overline E`; F5–`Λ` switch to decomposition of the discrete `E8` root set into two `H4` copies (lines 390–402, 606–612). The object being integrated changes mid-argument.
- F6 is called a proposition, labelled `thm:F6`, and then treated as if it supplied a smooth 4-manifold `S^3×R` or Minkowski lift already in F2 line 174. F6 proves no such thing.
- Line 527 uses `M_{μν}` (“where `M_{μν}` uplifts to `g_{μν}` by F7”) but `M_{μν}` is nowhere defined.
- Cross-references look syntactically present, but semantically some are misleading: line 119 says Section F6 “proves F6,” whereas the body itself admits the decisive rate step is not proved.
