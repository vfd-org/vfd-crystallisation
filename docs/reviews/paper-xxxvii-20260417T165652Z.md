**1. Claim audit**
- `Nu1` (`lines 105-110`): not established. The proof (`113-115`) does not show that all three physical neutrinos lie in `[134,145]`. Oscillation splittings plus `\Sigma m_\nu < 0.12 eV` do not impose a positive lower bound on the lightest eigenvalue; if one mass is arbitrarily small, the shell depth is arbitrarily larger than `145`. The claim silently assumes a nonzero lightest mass near the solar scale.
- `Nu1` numerical placement (`lines 98-103`): the three sample conversions are fine as arithmetic benchmarks, but they do not prove the band stated for the physical spectrum.
- `F5-Nu2` (`lines 137-141`): not established. The proof (`144-148`) just restates the hypothesis that the same “god-prime chirality” mechanism applies to neutrinos. That is not a proof of normal ordering; it is a conjectural transfer principle.

- Strong-CP corollary (`lines 241-245`): not established. `F5` gives a `σ`-intertwining statement for the closure functional on `σ`-fixed fields. That does not, by itself, imply vanishing of the low-energy QCD parameter `θ_QCD`, does not address the anomaly / `arg det M_q` issue, and does not justify the EFT statement “no axion is required.” The proof (`248-252`) is a category mistake between symmetry of the closure functional and elimination of a physical EFT parameter.
