**1. Claim Audit**

- `Proposition [Main]` (lines 142-159): the proof establishes only the tautological conditional “if a time-uniform `L^\infty` vorticity bound holds, BKM gives global continuation.” It does not establish the cascade bridge. The proof also weakens the stated data assumption from `s > 5/2` (line 149) to `s >= 1` (line 595).

- `Lemma [Pi_hyd ... sketch]` (lines 162-195): not proved. The sum is over `k in Z` (line 163), but the claimed geometric sum `sum_k phi^{-2k}=1+phi` (line 193) only holds for `k >= 0`. Over all integers it diverges. Also vorticity is one derivative of velocity, not “two derivatives” as stated at lines 202-204.

- Same lemma: the `L^\infty` bound is not obtained from the stated spectral norm `6+6phi` (lines 207-208). The graph Laplacian spectral/operator norm is an `L^2` operator norm; it does not give the required `L^\infty -> L^\infty` bound. For the graph Laplacian the row-sum bound would be different.

- `Theorem [F4]` (lines 323-334): as stated, this is only a dimension-count import. That is consistent with Paper XXXVI. But the paper later still uses “F4” as a bounded-spectrum input, e.g. lines 527-528 and 732-734. That is a misuse of F4.

- `Theorem [Cascade-NS correspondence]` (lines 367-384): not established. The proof is a verbal matching of terms, not a derivation. P7 only gives a conditional weak Navier-Stokes scaling limit on `S^3`, not a smooth `R^3` correspondence or inverse lift for arbitrary NS data.

The proof itself admits vortex stretching is rank-2 (lines 458-460), contradicting the “no uncontrolled vortex stretching” wording at lines 433-434.

- `Lemma [Bounded macroscopic operators]` (lines 465-473): overclaims. Item 1 calls energy “conserved” (line 470) while the displayed identity is dissipative. Items 2-3 rely on the later assumed `L^\infty` vorticity bound (lines 491-500); they are not independent consequences of cascade spectrum.

- `Corollary [Global extension]` (lines 572-590): valid only if `B_NS` holds with one constant `M` uniform in the terminal time `T`. That is exactly the open global-regularity bridge, so the corollary has no independent content.
