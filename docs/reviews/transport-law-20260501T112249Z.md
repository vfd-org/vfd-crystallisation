- Theorem `thm:u1-explicit` (lines 206–218): **not claim-audit-clean**. For $i\dot\psi=H\psi$,
  \[
  \dot\rho_v=2\operatorname{Im}(\bar\psi_v(H\psi)_v),

  not the negative sign used at line 223. With the current as defined at lines 208–210, the stated continuity equation has the wrong sign.
