**Claim Audit**
All line numbers refer to `papers/paper-lix/paper-lix.tex`.

- L54-L58, “The ladder … is thereby derived”: not established in this paper. No proof or citation is supplied for the exact φ-recursive refinement tower, the coherence-length law, or the inverse mass-length identification. This should be a conditional proposition unless the upstream theorem is cited precisely.
- L60-L62, “The muon’s shell is exactly … 96”: acceptable only as a hypothesis. Paper LII uses shell 96, but does not independently derive it; its own coefficient is pending/estimated. LIX also omits LVII’s caveat that non-circularity is only internal-programme provenance.
- L68-L92, mass table: numerics match `scripts/sm_ledger.py`. But these are postdictions after reading integer shells from data, not predictions. “Parameter-free” is only true after the placed integers are supplied.
- L100-L102, curvature obstruction: valid for additive separable rules `N=a_T+f(g)`. It does not refute arbitrary “static quantum numbers plus generation” rules with interactions or nonlinear type-generation coupling.
- L104-L106, budget proposition: per-type affine failure is established. “Cheapest exact class” is only true within per-type polynomial models. The 25-bit threshold is a protocol choice, not a mathematical theorem; “numerology by definition” is rhetoric.
- L108-L110, sieve scan: verified for the eight tested sieves under the stated hypergeometric/Bonferroni protocol. “The one surviving non-dynamical class” is too broad; only this tested sieve family survives the earlier obstruction.
- L112-L114, one-loop/log and regression: established for the specific one-parameter monotone lepton form and the reported regression. It does not exclude general loop dressing.
- L124-L126, “Not finer rungs”: the p-values reproduce. The conclusion is too strong: the tests show no evidence for integer/half/quarter grids, not that intermediate rungs of every geometric kind are absent.
- L128-L130, W/Z: correct as on-shell electroweak bookkeeping. “Identically” should be scoped to the on-shell/tree-level mass relation and definition of `sin^2 theta_W`.
- L132-L134, scheme/binding: the monotone means are verified. The causal language is not proved; the paragraph is correctly labelled observation, but the section title calls the attributions “derived.”
- L142-L144, atoms: overclaimed. The calculation is the nonrelativistic Coulomb/infinite-proton-mass model. It does not make the measured hydrogen spectrum “exact”; reduced mass, fine structure, Lamb shift, hyperfine effects, and the conditional alpha error remain.
