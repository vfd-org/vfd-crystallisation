**1. Claim Audit**
- L151-168: the main proposition is only a tautology after assuming `B_BSD,rank`, `B_BSD,spec`, and implicitly `B_BSD,conv`. The statement omits `B_BSD,conv`, although `T_E:=T_E(1)` is not defined without it.

- L233-250: Proposition `Termwise operator bound` is false as stated. The proof gives `≤ 2φ log p`, then “absorbs” `φ` into `≤2 log p`; that is not a valid inequality. Also `v_φ(p)` is not defined rigorously: `φ` is a unit in `Z[φ]`, so “φ-adic valuation of p” is not a standard valuation.
- L265-280: the convergence theorem is salvageable with the corrected constant and a defined operator domain. But L280 is false: `Σ_p log p p^{-s}` is not equal to `-ζ'(s)/ζ(s)`; the latter includes prime powers.
- L283-290: the numerical bound is inconsistent with the script output. The script gives Hasse upper bound `10.52587` at `s=1` and `0.63763` at `s=2`; with the `φ` factor, the second displayed bound should be about `0.64 φ`, not `0.32 φ`.
- L358-366: the Eichler-Shimura theorem is oversimplified. It is not literally a Hecke-equivariant isomorphism from `H^1(E,Q)` to the displayed modular-form space without specifying the modular parametrisation/newform quotient and coefficient conventions.

- L437-456 and L475-478: `thm:specL` is a remark explicitly saying the spectral identity is not proved, but the proof cites it as “Theorem”. The proof only works after assuming the bridge axiom.
- L586-604: the 600-cell spectral count is locally verified by the cited script: `94/120 = 78.3%`, paired multiplicity `26/120`, top eigenvalue `6+6φ`. This claim is established as a finite spectral computation only.
- L606-613: the transfer to BSD is not established. The additional condition “if `T_E` is a polynomial in `L`” is unsupported and points to the wrong section.
