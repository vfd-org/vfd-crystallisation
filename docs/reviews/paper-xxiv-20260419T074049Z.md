**Claim Audit**

- **Relativity of simultaneity**: “If events \(e_1,e_2\) are incomparable … there exist observers \(O_1,O_2\) such that \(t_{O_1}(e_1)<t_{O_1}(e_2)\) and \(t_{O_2}(e_2)<t_{O_2}(e_1)\)” ([paper-xxiv.tex](/mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxiv/paper-xxiv.tex:112), lines 112-121).

- **Time-dilation analogue**: “If \(\mu_1\) and \(\mu_2\) differ on the set of events lying between \(e_1\) and \(e_n\) in the shared ordering, then \(\Delta t_{O_1}(C)\neq \Delta t_{O_2}(C)\)” ([paper-xxiv.tex](/mnt/c/Users/nexus/OneDrive/Documents/My Projects/vfd-crystalisation-paper/papers/paper-xxiv/paper-xxiv.tex:138), lines 138-152).

  \]
  or to a pointwise dominance condition on \(S\). As stated, the theorem is false.
