**Claim Audit**
- `L142-160` Main proposition: only follows after assuming `B_NS`, which is essentially the required uniform BKM vorticity bound. The proof does not establish `H_1`, `H_NS`, or `H_P6/P7`; it packages them.
- `L162-195` `\Pi_hyd` lemma: not established. The sum over `k in Z` diverges; the later `k >= 0` sum is miscomputed: `sum_{k>=0} phi^{-2k} = phi`, not `1+phi`. Vorticity is one derivative, not two. The proof also uses a Shannon/sinc reconstruction on a non-lattice finite 600-cell substrate without hypotheses proving sampling, partition of unity, divergence-free compatibility, or viscous compatibility.
- `L315-346` F2/F4/F5 imports: F4 is correctly demoted to a dimension count. F5 is correctly conditional/discrete. F2 is still overbroad: Paper XXXVI classifies a restricted symmetric rank-2, order-≤2, degree-≤2 sector under H-MIN; it does not prove “no rank-3 or higher” content in the sense later used for NS.
- `L367-415` Cascade-NS correspondence: not proved. The proof is a hand-wave from variational terms to NS. The term assignment is internally inconsistent: `R` gives convection at `L378` but Laplacian plus advection at `L398-399`; `Q` gives viscosity at `L380` but material derivative and viscosity at `L402-403`. This must be a hypothesis, not a theorem.
- `L417-423` water-viscosity numerical check: no calculation, citation, units derivation, or error estimate. Unsupported.
- `L429-460` rank preservation: proof invalid. Linear coarse-graining does not control nonlinear differential operators; “rank” of tensor expressions is not a bound on vortex stretching. The conclusion “must be bounded by spectrum” is not proved.
- `L465-500` bounded macroscopic operators: item (1) is the standard smooth energy equality, not “energy is conserved.” Items (2)-(3) depend on the later `L^\infty` vorticity hypothesis and therefore are not proved here.
- `L506-518` `B_NS`: acceptable as a hypothesis, but it is exactly the missing regularity input.
- `L561-590` BKM/global extension: the corollary follows from `B_NS` plus a valid NS continuation criterion. But the cited BKM paper is Euler-titled; use an explicit NS continuation reference or state this as a standard BKM-type NS criterion with citation.
- `L594-618` proof of main proposition: formal only. `s >= 1` at `L595` contradicts `s > 5/2` at `L149`, and Step 2 relies on the unproved Cascade-NS theorem.
- `L636-685` spectral/dynamical diagnostics: the 600-cell spectrum and `94/120` count check out locally. The transfer to vorticity support or `H_1` does not. R2/R3 in the dynamics script are forced by explicitly applying `P_sigma`; they are not independent evidence that natural closure dynamics selects it.
- `L689-695` aria H4O claim: correctly says external and not locally reverified. This conflicts with `L252-261`, which presents the same family of claims as established support.
