**1. Claim Audit**
- `bsd-formal.tex:136`: “Conditional on ... B_BSD,rank ... and B_BSD,spec ... BSD ... holds.” The proof only works if `B_BSD,conv` is also assumed, since `T_E(1)` and its spectrum are otherwise undefined. Hidden hypothesis.

- `bsd-formal.tex:250`: “Absolute convergence on Re(s)>1.” The convergence claim is plausible with corrected constants, but the proof depends on the faulty proposition. Also `sum_p log p p^{-s}` is not equal to `-ζ'/ζ(s)`; that includes all prime powers.
