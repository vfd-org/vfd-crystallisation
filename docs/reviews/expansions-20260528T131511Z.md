**Verdict**

PUBLICATION READY: **NO**. The body now mostly fixes the A1-vs-Laplian eigenvalue confusion, but the paper still overstates several cross-paper identifications and retains publication-blocking ambiguity around the “sigma-paired dipole class.”

**1. Claim Audit**

- `expansions.tex:50-55`: Paper 1 establishes the 120-vertex 600-cell scheme, nine eigenspaces, closure operator, and norm-map role. Mostly supported by Paper 1. The norm-map universality is imported/classical, not proved de novo here.

- `expansions.tex:59-65`: “94-dimensional sigma-fixed family and 13-dimensional sigma-paired class anchor cascade finite-level refinement infrastructure.” Not established. Paper 1 supports `94` and one-sided `13 = dim(E1)+dim(E2)`, but the cascade paper’s `28`-dimensional fixed space lives in its auxiliary fibre `F0`, not in Paper 1’s sigma-fixed/sigma-paired eigenspaces.

- `expansions.tex:67-70`: cosmology uses a sigma-paired class as Lambda/H0 substrate. Partly supported. The cosmology paper uses the `+6φ` dipole eigenspace and the `L + A = dI` complement. This does not justify the later identification with all of `E1 \oplus E8`.

- `expansions.tex:72-76`: Note G “predicts” the volatile anaesthetic correlation `rho=+0.94`, `n=11`. The numerical statement is present in Note G, but the source explicitly frames it as a consistency check, not an independent prediction.

- `expansions.tex:78-83`: “The λ=12 eigenspace of Paper 1 (multiplicity 1, all-ones) and λ=0 eigenspace ... carry the Schläfli bridge.” Still misleading. The bridge is global Laplacian `λ_L=12`, i.e. Paper 1 adjacency `λ_A=0`, `E4`, dimension 25. The all-ones space is Laplacian `0`, not the bridge carrier.

- `expansions.tex:85-90`: `tau_ico` fixed dimension 94 and `tau_spec` fixed dimension 116. Supported by the existence paper. But “structurally grounded” is too strong for `tau_spec`, which is a separate convention, not derived in Paper 1.

- `expansions.tex:159-166`: cascade statement. `V600` at the `H4` rung and the `28`-dimensional `W(H4)`-fixed fibre are supported by the cascade-selection paper. “40-vertex root system” is wrong: the cited existence/cascade material treats `40` as a Schläfli rank-count/refinement rung, not a 40-vertex root system.

- `expansions.tex:170-185`: the paper now correctly says the `28`-dimensional fibre is separate and no intertwiner is given. The eigenvalue identity `9-3√5 = 12-6φ` is correct. “Organise the lowest cascade band” remains interpretive, not proved.

- `expansions.tex:200-214`: cosmology connection. The Planck-level numerical matches are externally present. The claim that the relevant class is `E1 \oplus E8` and that the substrate constant is Paper 1’s `C_phi` restricted to that pair is not established. Paper 1 has `C_phi = L + φ^{-2}I`; the cosmology source uses a separate quadratic correction built from `L`, `A`, and `Q`.

- `expansions.tex:218-221`: “not an empirical coincidence but a structural prediction of the triad.” Over-claim. The cited source reports agreement; this paper does not prove that the numerical success follows from Paper 1.

- `expansions.tex:229-244`: biology connection. The `rho=+0.94`, `n=11`, and H-MT-Bridge ratio claims are present in Note G. The identification of Note G’s class with `E1 \oplus E8` is not: Note G uses the 4-dimensional `+6φ` dipole eigenspace. The T15 residue statement is also not supported; Note G says V600 leverage is uniform and the biological node ranking is downstream symmetry breaking.

- `expansions.tex:248-251`: “evidence for a Paper 1-derived prediction.” Not supported. The source says the correlation is not an independent test.

- `expansions.tex:259-270`: spectral bridge. The five 24-cell cosets, local `λ_L=12` lift into global `λ_L=12`, `dim 25`, and `A5` character split are supported. The notation `2T, g2T, ..., g^4 2T` is stronger than the source: the source uses five right cosets, not necessarily powers of one coset generator.

- `expansions.tex:272-286`: Laplacian convention is now correct. This is the strongest Round 2 repair.

- `expansions.tex:288-290`: “`W(H4)`-equivariant projection onto `E4`” is not established by the bridge sources. They prove zero-extension into `E4` and `A5`/binary-icosahedral stability, not a `W(H4)` projection theorem.

- `expansions.tex:308-340`: existence section. The dimensions `94` and `116` are supported. - `expansions.tex:347-349`: “every quantitative claim ... follows from Paper 1.” False. Several quantitative claims are imported from cosmology, biology, bridge, and cascade papers, and some identifications do not follow from Paper 1.
