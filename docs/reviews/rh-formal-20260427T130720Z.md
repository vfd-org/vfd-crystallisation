**1. Claim Audit**

Round 3 σ-attractor fixes in `subsec:attractor` are mostly substantively closed. The fake operator-σ has been removed, the `V_{\mathrm{fix}}/V_{\mathrm{pair}}` split is now explicit, `R3` is honestly called non-control, and the sign convention `σ(φ)=1-φ=-1/φ` is fixed. Major claim failures:

- Lines 451-458, “F5: σ-invariance”: not established by the cited current source. `cascade-foundations.md` says F5 is superseded, and `paper-xxxvi.tex` reformulates F5 as a conditional factor-2 statement under H-SIG, not `F[σΦ]=F[Φ]` for all fields.

- Lines 481-494, rational-prime recovery: partially fixed, but still false as written. `5 = φ(φ-1)(-1)` is false, and “`p = N_{K/Q}(π)` for any prime `π|p`” fails for inert primes, where the prime ideal over `p` has norm `p^2`.

- Lines 639-648, “`widehatζ(s)=widehatζ(1-s)`”: false for the stated uncompleted rational function. With `q=z^{10}`, `F(q^{-1})=q^{24}F(q)`. The pole set is reciprocal; the function is not invariant unless completed, e.g. by `q^{12}F(q)`.

- Lines 653-685, Hecke obstruction: conclusion is plausible, but proof is not adequate. `widehatζ` is a finite Artin-Mazur rational function, not a prime-indexed Euler product, so assigning split-prime values `φ^K` is an extra interpretation not defined in the paper.

- Lines 704-729, H4O dynamical witness: this is external empirical reporting, not theorem-grade content in this paper. Also “positive Lyapunov exponent `+0.00384`” does not support “non-chaotic, integrable” without a precise convention.

- Lines 752-780, B14: still overclaims. The adjacency-Laplian vs velocity-covariance distinction is now fixed, but “8 σ-paired poles” is not derived from the multiplicity-weighted `K`-multiset `{72:1,0:1,52:5,20:5}`; counting distinct factors gives 4 pair types, counting multiplicity gives 12. Line 774, “Both spectra have multiplicity sum 120,” is not verified for the external velocity-covariance spectrum.

- Lines 910-973, construction of `ζ_cas`: the text says “cascade-derived Dirichlet series,” but the definition simply sets `ζ_cas` equal to the classical Euler product and its analytic continuation.

- Lines 985-1025, functional equation: proof fails. The embedded “Riemann’s functional equation for Euler products” is not true for general Euler products, and F5 does not supply a theta transformation. Minimal fix: import the classical functional equation because `ζ_cas` was defined as `ζ`.

- Lines 1205-1223, zeros-resonances bijection: not verified. The cited local `CascadeRH` file has no Proposition 4.1 and contains older overclaims; this must be a conjecture or removed.

- Lines 1227-1237, `F|_{H4}` commutes with σ: not supported by current F5, and conflicts with lines 1329-1349, which correctly say σ does not act on `R^{120}` as an operator commuting with `L`.

- Lines 1588-1605, “σ acts as `s -> 1-s`” under the `φ`-Mellin transform: proof relies on the invalid `φ`-adic valuation. It is valid for formal monomials `φ^a`, not for the stated Mellin transform on `R_+`.
